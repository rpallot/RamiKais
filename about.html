I love all creative processes. This
is the root of my affection and
continuing infatuation with design.
I enjoy creating things, and the
subsequent lessons and experiences
that are engendered from the
process. Problem solving is an integral
element to the human psyche, and
design solves problems; with an
intrinsic aim of augmenting social

Melbourne, Australia and work as
a freelance design consultant. Please
feel free to get in touch, via the contact
details provided, if my services can
assist you in any way.
