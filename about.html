I love all creative processes. This is the root of my affection and continuing infatuation with design. I enjoy creating things, and the subsequent lessons and experiences that are engendered from the process. Problem solving is an integral element to the human psyche, and design solves problems; with an intrinsic aim of augmenting social value.
